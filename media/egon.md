## Egon.io — the domain lens

Capture **domain stories** in the language of the people who live the domain: actors,
work objects and the activities that connect them, told as a story.

Files use the `.egn` extension and open in the Egon.io Domain Story Modeler.

**Open the Egon.io tutorial** (the button on the left) for a guided
"create your first story" walkthrough from the modeler itself.
