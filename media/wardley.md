## Wardley Maps — the strategic lens

Wardley Maps position the components of your value chain against how far they have
**evolved** (from novel to commodity), so you can reason about *where to invest* and
*how things will change*.

Files use the `.wmap` (or `.owm`) extension and open in a dedicated map editor.

**Open the Wardley Maps tutorial** (the button on the left) for a guided
"create your first map" walkthrough from the modeler itself.
