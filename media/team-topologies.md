## Team Topologies — the organization lens

Model your organization with the four fundamental **team types** and the three
**interaction modes**, so you can see how teams are shaped and how they collaborate
to evolve your architecture.

Files use the `.tt` extension and open in a dedicated diagram editor.

**Open the Team Topologies tutorial** (the button on the left) for a guided
"create your first diagram" walkthrough from the modeler itself.
